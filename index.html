Why We Exist

Texas’ first responders face hazards daily—from wildfires to medical crises. Yet many departments lack funding for the latest gear and mental health resources.

Our mission: Unite Texans to ensure every responder has the support, equipment, and training they deserve.

FAQs

Are donations tax-deductible?

We are applying for 501(c)(3) status. All funds go to vetted Texas responder programs.

How quickly does support arrive?

Within 3-4 weeks, with delivery updates via email.

Can I choose where my gift goes?

Yes—add a note at checkout specifying gear, training, or wellness support.
